Programming music - WikipediaProgramming musicFrom Wikipedia the free encyclopediaJump to navigationJump to searchNot to be confused with program musicProgramming is a form of music production and performance using electronic devices and computer software such as sequencers and workstations or hardware synthesizers sampler and sequencers to generate sounds of musical instruments Programming has been used in most electronic music and hip hop music since the 1990s It is also frequently used in modern pop and rock music from various regions of the world and sometimes in jazz and contemporary classical musicMusic programming is the process in which a musician produces a sound or patch be it from scratch or with the aid of a synthesizersampler or uses a sequencer to arrange a song
